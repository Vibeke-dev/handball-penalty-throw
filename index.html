PENALTY THROW -- HANDBALL

Do you want to win the Handball trophy of
European Championship 2022

Rules:

  • Move Landin/goalkeeper by up/down left/right arrows on the keyboard
  • The ball will be thrown when you press on the Danish flag button in the right side of the screen
  • One round is best out of 5 balls - if you win you move up to the next level
  • If you complete all 5 levels, you are truly a champion

HOW TO PLAY:

  • Use 'Throw Ball' button above to throw ball

  • Follow score in game area

  • Move by left/right arrows on keyboard
